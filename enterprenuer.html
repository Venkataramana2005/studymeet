Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join a class?

To join a class, simply search for the class in the "Classes" section and click the "Join Class" button.

Can I download notes shared by others?

Yes, you can browse and download notes from the "Shared Notes" section after searching by subject or topic.

How do I contact the instructor?

You can contact instructors through the "Class Details" page. Look for the instructor's profile and messaging options.

Help Center

How do I enroll in a class?

To enroll in a class, navigate to the "Classes" section, select the class you're interested in, and click "Enroll." If it's a paid class, you'll be prompted to make a payment.

How do I upload notes?

Go to your class dashboard, and under the "Materials" section, click "Upload Notes." You can upload files in PDF or DOC format.

How can I track my progress?

You can track your progress in the "Progress Tracker" section on your profile dashboard. Progress is tracked by completion percentage for each class you're enrolled in.
